About IEEE SBCEK WIE

Established in 2010, IEEE SBCEK WIE is one of the oldest WIE affinity groups in Kerala. It provides a platform for women students to innovate, collaborate, and grow into global leaders in engineering and technology.

Mission

To inspire, engage, and advance women in STEM at CEK and beyond by connecting them with mentors, leaders, and opportunities through IEEE WIE Global.

Vision

To foster a future where women engineers at CEK and worldwide lead with confidence, creativity, and impact.

Why Join?

  • Connect with global IEEE WIE
  • Access leadership programs
  • Skill-building workshops
  • Networking & mentorship
